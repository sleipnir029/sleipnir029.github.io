# Boighor

Boighor (বইঘর) is a non-profit digital lending library. Readers borrow books, read them in-browser, and return them - the way a physical library works, but online and open to anyone with a slow connection.

For the full story behind why I built it, see the launch post: [Why I Built Boighor](/blog/why-i-built-boighor-a-library-for-everyone-everywhere).
