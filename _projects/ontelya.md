# Ontelya

Ontelya helps researchers stop copy-pasting from PDFs. Upload a paper, parse it into structured data, apply reusable extraction schemas, verify findings through human review, and produce versioned datasets linked back to the original source.

Built for meta-analyses, systematic reviews, and collaborative data extraction where reproducibility and source attribution are non-negotiable.

Visit the live site at [ontelya.com](https://ontelya.com/).
