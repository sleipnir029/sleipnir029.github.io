<h2>Life Story</h2>

I've always had a knack for **making things**, most of the times by breaking them. This continued for long enough to carry me through an engineering discipline. I'm a **materials science** major at **BUET**. Here I discovered the joy of making things without breaking them, almost, through **programming**.

I started to write programs, mostly with bugs to automate little things. Moreover, I was always fascinated with **games** and how they work. Eventually, I made a few. It led me to many rabbit holes and soon discovered a different universe altogether in programming. So, now I'm trying my best to understand its ins and outs.
